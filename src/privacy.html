Privacy Policy

Hipflask collects the following data from the Facebook API:

  • Your user details (including your name, email and location)
  • Events you are attending
  • Your friends list
  • Events your friends are attending

Hipflask stores this data in our database in order to:

  • Recommend events based on your behaviour and that of your friends
  • Use the data and metadata in aggregate to provide insights into how to make Hipflask better for users and;
  • Recommend featured events to users by providing access to aggregate data (e.g. bar graphs and pie charts) to third-parties
  • Speed up your user experience by minimising calls to facebook

We will never:

  • Allow direct access to the Hipflask database to any third party except when required by the laws of Australia
  • We will do our very best not to let this happen (ala PRISM in the US)
  • Compromise on our efforts to keep your data secure. Hipflask uses security best practices regarding encryption, passwords, API keys and access control
